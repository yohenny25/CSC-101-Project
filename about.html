ABOUT SWEETS SHOP

The Sweets Shop was founded in New York by two college classmates during
the summer of 2020.This shop first began on a small business page on the Instagram
social media platform ran by the founders themselves at the time. As the business grew
bigger and bigger, the founder decided to open up her own local shop, in where the
clientele that she was able to obtain from the social media page, can come in to
buy and eat their sweets, as well as new clients.
