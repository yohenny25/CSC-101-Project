ABOUT SWEETS SHOP

The Sweets Shop was founded in New York City by Ms. Delacruz during the
summer of 2019. This shop first began as a small business page on the Instagram
social media platform ran by the founder herself at the time. As the business grew
bigger and bigger, the founder decided to open up her own local shop, in where the
customers that she was able to obtain from the social media page, can come in to
buy and eat their sweets, as well as new customers.
